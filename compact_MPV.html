Definition

A compact MPV is fitting between the mini MPV and large MPV sub-segments. In Japan, the term "space wagon" is sometimes used to refer to this type of vehicle. Compact MPVs have better "flexibility" than other body styles: seats may be individually folded or even removed, the bonnet may be shorter and the passengers sit more upright than in regular cars, providing for a roomier interior.
