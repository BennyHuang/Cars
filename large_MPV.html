Definition

Also known as “people carriers” and "minivan", this class of cars resembles tall estate cars. Larger MPVs may have seating for up to eight passengers. Being taller than a family car improves visibility for the driver and may help access for the elderly or disabled. They also offer more seats and increased load capacity than hatchbacks or estate cars.
